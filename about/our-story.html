Be Present

Circa 2010

Be Present. Check out this t-shirt. It embodies our “why”:

Be Present. What you see in-front of you are the hands of a beast jutting out from someone’s torso, and holding a phone. What does this have to do with “Be Present?” Everything.

The idea originated when our CEO, Matt Keiser, was spending an afternoon with his kids. He realized that he was spending more time on his phone working than he was spending time interacting with his kids. A life-changing moment. He wasn’t himself when he had his phone out. It actually wasn’t him. It was the monster within. An alter ego. He was with his family but he wasn’t present. And he realized quickly that he needed to change. So he embraced that moment and created a reminder of the importance of being present.

He created this shirt to remind everyone at LiveIntent to “Be Present”. The lesson had broader applications, which is why when you come to one of our offices you’ll see the motto and the logo reflected everywhere. In essence, “Be Present” has three layers.

Be Present

with your peers and colleagues.

Turn your phones off. Close your laptops. When you’re in a meeting, of two or twenty, be present in that meeting and give your peers the respect and attention you would expect in return.

Be Present

with your family and your friends.

We aim to ensure that we engage each in a way that allows the to become better every day

Be Present

or our customers. Advertisers. Publishers. Brands.

Fun people. Dedicated people. Smart people. People-people. And together we do awesome stuff. No diggity!

The T-Shirt is our most popular. And it’s a reminder to anyone wearing it or seeing it pass by them in the hallway, to “Be Present.” In all three ways.

Come be present us!
