What is LiveIntent?

You’re on our website. You’re looking at our “Careers” page. This is an opportunity we don’t want to waste. So let’s cut to the chase.

We can sell you on LiveIntent with more words than Joyce needed for Ulysses but that’s not going to change your mind. It’s not going to move you from “researcher (nudge nudge wink wink)” to “candidate.”

The only thing that’s going to convince you to share your resume with us is a belief in our story and our vision, an appreciation and interest in what we’ve accomplished, followed by a visit to one of our offices to interview with some of our teams. Ideally, when that happens, you have an honest-to-goodness realization that what you want to do in your career maps perfectly well to what we’re trying to do as a company … and how we’re trying to do it. Because the how matters just as much (if not more) than the what.

If you want to browse around a bit, feel free to take a look at some photos and some fluffy feel good language below. But there’s no substitute for a face-to-face conversation for the both of us. There’s no better way to realize whether or not LiveIntent is the right place for you. We’re always down for a chat if you are?

Our People, Our Success

This is a picture of people dressed up in costumes. it’s not at our office. It’s at one of the after party’s at one of our semi-annual all company offsites. We fly people from all of our offices around the world in to our HQ in NYC for an entire week. A bunch of time is spent in the office just doing our day-to-day work, but doing it without things like the Atlantic and Pacific Ocean between us. And 1-2 days are spent in structured sessions where we talk about everything from our values and our priorities, to educational topics like “Crucial Conversations" that make us a better team.

And sometimes (usually) we close out the week with a party that’s envy inspiring. And sometimes (often) we have ridiculous themes and dress up in support of them. This time was a luau at Royal Palms. If you want to be a part of this picture, join LiveIntent, start dating someone at LiveIntent, or “crush” photoshop and insert yourself.

Our benefits and perks.

We want to ensure LiveIntenter’s come into work every morning and leave everyday feeling supported and balanced in their lives. Our benefits and perks are geared to enable employees to be successful and happy.

Healthcare and Transit

LiveIntent provides comprehensive health insurance options, including dental and vision, 401(k) contributions and pre-tax commuter benefits.

Fitness and Mindfulness

Our in-house fitness programs- yoga, zumba and pilates- help us stay in shape and clear our heads. We also have a discounted gym membership.

Staying Fueled

We offer healthy snack options every afternoon to keep you fueled. Working late? Order dinner on us with a company seamless account.

Always Learning

Grow presentation (and listening) skills with our personal Toastmaster’s Chapter, as well as take advantage of online classes and tutorials the company offers.
