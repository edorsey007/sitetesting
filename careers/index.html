We're all about people.

What is LiveIntent?

LiveIntent was born and brewed in downtown Manhattan in 2009. Since then, a lot has changed, but our core principles have stayed the same. We love email and shout it from the rooftops. We’re passionate about educating our clients, partners and employees so that they love email, too. We walk through walls to solve problems and work tirelessly to combat the misconception about “the death of email.”

We have a good time.

Beyond the Color Runs, the volleyball team, the cooking classes, the paint nights, and the Escape the Rooms, we look for the fun in solving problems. It’s all too easy for CPMs, ad serving, and discrepancies to get dull, so we truly try to bring fun to whatever we’re doing. Whether it’s a spontaneous costume party, a volunteering opportunity, or a friendly chat around the snack table at work, we help each other and share lots of laughs.

Get to know us.

We’re smart. We're unique. We’re fun to work with. And we’re looking for people that are smart, unique, and fun to work with. We’ve also been recognized by Forbes as one of the most promising companies, and by Crain’s as the 2nd fastest growing company in NY (for 2 years straight!). If this seems like a rocket ship you want to get on board, we’d love to hear from you.

Our benefits and perks.

We want to ensure LiveIntenters come into work every morning and leave everyday feeling supported and balanced in their lives. Our benefits and perks are geared to enable employees to be successful and happy.

Healthcare and Transit

LiveIntent provides comprehensive health insurance options, including dental and vision, 401(k) contributions and pre-tax commuter benefits.

Fitness and Mindfulness

Our in-house fitness programs- yoga, zumba and pilates- help us stay in shape and clear our heads. We also have a discounted gym membership.

Staying Fueled

We offer healthy snack options every afternoon to keep you fueled. Working late? Order dinner on us with a company seamless account.

Always Learning

Grow presentation (and listening) skills with our personal Toastmaster’s Chapter, as well as take advantage of online classes and tutorials the company offers.
