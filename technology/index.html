Our Technology Story

Email, the internet’s workhorse from very beginning.

91% of consumers use it at least once a day (ExactTarget citation) and it’s 40x better at acquiring new customers than Facebook and Twitter (McKinsey citation.)

More people are spending more time online than ever before -- across all screens, devices, and browsers. In this new, cross-device world, email is more important than ever. While most digital media was lauding “real-time,” and “programmatic”, advertising within email seemed more like direct mail than digital marketing. Targeting was nonexistent, cookies were obsolete and optimization was scant. Advertisers paid for email ads on send. A publisher partner would hardcode the ad into an email template for each and every send. There was no transparency on what kind of delivery the advertiser would receive, nor reporting on how it performed.

LiveIntent knows that your email subscribers are your most valuable customers – they have opted-in and given you permission to message them. They expect and deserve only the best user experience possible. Our LiveTag is helping make that possible.

The LiveTag

Everything on the LiveIntent Platform begins and ends with the LiveTag. This snippet of lightweight HTML code is compatible with all ESP’s and allows email senders to monetize their email newsletters and alerts. In doing so, it connects to our robust prediction engine, which enables advertisers to reach the exact target audience they’re looking for, in the best performing media channel. Most importantly, it enables 130MM people to get the right message, at the right time, on the right device. How’s that for the best of both the tech & data worlds?

Publishers and brands only need to add the LiveTag to their email templates once to monetize their email newsletters easily and effectively. Once a person opens an email newsletter that contains a LiveTag, LiveIntent’s servers detect a number of variables and chooses the best advertisement, marketing message, or content to show, in real-time. No more hard coding ads into every single newsletter send.

The limits of email - with hundreds of permutations of device, operating system, and browser - have yielded some interesting benefits. As a result of these complications, LiveTags are as lean as possible, no heavy javascript or invasive iframes, including only the code that is absolutely necessary to serve messages into email.

What makes this all possible? The power of the email address.

The Email Address

The LiveIntent platform is powered by the only digital ID tied to a real person: the email address. The email address is a universal identifier, long-lived and unique to an individual.

It used to just be a place you received messages, but is now the way you identify yourself, both on and offline. You use your email address to log-in, just about everywhere: purchases, social networks, and apps. That’s why our inventory is over 99% real people. We work with over 1100 premium publishers like the New York Times, Kraft, Walmart, and more, to unlock this amazing audience.

The power of the email address enables us to target the right ad to the right person, right when they’re paying attention — the moment they open an email. Even better? LiveIntent doesn’t rely on PII. Using the secure, anonymized, de-identified version of that email address — the email hash — as a unique identifier enables the ability to target people (not pixels) across devices and attribute online to offline behavior. Using the hash, LiveIntent is able to find and target defined users across every screen, device, and browser — so that you’re always reaching the intended audience.

The Data (Science)

The LiveIntent Platform reaches a whopping 130MM people – 2nd only to Facebook for online addressable audience. Leveraging the power of the email address as a person’s unique identifier, LiveIntent’s Platform can recognize a user across screens for consistent marketing — irrespective of device. Processing hundreds of data points through through 200 million auctions per day creates an immense amount of data to provide our advertisers, publishers, brands, and end users the best experience possible.

LiveIntent is committed to building on this data responsibly, securely, and effectively to reach consumers across all platforms with most relevant messaging possible. Together, we can offer brands and advertisers the ability to map customer intent data to our audience of over 130MM people so that they can reach these customers with more relevant marketing and advertising when engaging with emails sent by 1100 of the world’s largest brands and publishers.

The Developers Community

LiveIntent has engineering teams, ranging from New York City to Copenhagen to Berlin, requiring design patterns that allow them to work as autonomously and efficiently as possible. As a result, the LiveIntent Platform utilizes API's that makes it accessible to other applications, including external partners and customers. We aim to make our APIs clean, self-documenting, and RESTful in order for developers to be excited about using them and to share them with others. Among other best practices, we version the API in the URL, use JSON whenever possible, and use Swagger for documentation.
