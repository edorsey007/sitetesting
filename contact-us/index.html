About LiveIntent

The LiveIntent platform helps 900+ top brands deliver marketing and advertising messages to an attention-rich audience of 130MM+ people each month, in emails opened by the loyal subscribers of 1100+ top US publishers and brands. In 2010, LiveIntent cut its teeth as a smarter way to buy and sell ads in email and has since been recognized as the second fastest growing company in NYC (Crain’s, 2014 & 2015) and as one of America’s Most Promising Companies (Forbes 2015.)
