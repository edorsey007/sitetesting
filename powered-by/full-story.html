Why am I getting ads in my email?

LiveIntent is a technology platform that publishers and brands use to buy and sell ads in email more easily and efficiently.

However, LiveIntent does not actually produce any of the content in the email or send any email.

If you want to stop seeing ads in your emails, please contact the publisher sending the email.
